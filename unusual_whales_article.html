I you’ve spent any time playing around with AI tools lately, you’ve probably run into the same problem most people do. The AI can talk about markets, it can explain concepts, but the moment you ask it for anything real, it starts guessing due to no real access to live market data.

So we fixed that.

We just gave Claude and other AI agents access to the entire options and stock market through the Unusual Whales MCP Server. This is a live, direct connection that lets AI assistants pull real market data whenever they need it; not a demo environment.

Once connected to our MCP Server, your AI can pull the same types of data traders use every day:

Options flow hitting the tape in real time. Sweeps and block trades. Dark pool prints alongside NBBO context. The kinds of signals traders normally have to piece together from multiple tools. It can also pull congressional trading activity, full financial statements for public companies, and institutional disclosures like 13F filings and insider trades.

If you want to work with technical indicators, do it!

RSI, MACD, VWAP, Bollinger Bands, and others. You can screen thousands of tickers by IV rank, put/call ratio, short interest, or other parameters.

You can even look at Greek exposure by strike, and map out the overall net options premiums across the entire market.

You can even look at all Polymarket data and find the insiders you want.

In other words, once your AI is connected, it isn’t guessing anymore, it’s working with actual market data.

From there you can build whatever you want.

Some people are already using it to build trading bots that monitor options flow throughout the day. Others are building dashboards that track the specific signals they care about. You can build screeners, alert systems, research tools, or anything else that benefits from structured market data.
