# Market as Recursive System (FRACTAL FUTURE Lens)

## Thesis
Markets exhibit nested structure across timeframes:
- microstructure → intraday → daily → weekly → macro regime

Patterns repeat, but parameters shift (volatility, liquidity, sentiment, policy).

## Fractal Principles Applied
- **Scale coherence:** a signal should behave consistently across timeframes OR be explicitly bounded.
- **Recursive learning:** strategy improvement loops via observe → test → refine.
- **Graceful failure:** local strategy failure must not cascade to portfolio collapse.
- **Resonance over control:** trade with regime, not against it.
- **Entropy pressure:** volatility is energy dispersion; correlation is entropy coupling; regime change is entropy phase transition.

## Operational Translation
- Regime detection is mandatory context for any signal.
- Strategy = (Signal) × (Regime Filter) × (Risk Packet) × (Execution Rules)
- Entropy regime (E1–E5) governs posture and sizing.
