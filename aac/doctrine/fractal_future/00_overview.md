# AAC × FRACTAL FUTURE — Overview (v1.0)

## Purpose
Integrate FRACTAL FUTURE into AAC as an operational lens for:
1) Signal research & regime detection (multi-scale coherence)
2) Risk architecture (fail-local, blast-radius containment)

## Core Assertion
Markets are recursive, feedback-driven systems. Strategies that endure preserve coherence across scale and contain failure locally.

## Non-Negotiables
- No signal is "valid" until it passes multi-scale validation or is explicitly labeled bounded.
- No strategy is allowed to risk portfolio-level collapse (fail-local is mandatory).
- All claims must be operationally defined and evidence-labeled.

## Evidence Labels (AAC Mapping)
- E0: conceptual hypothesis
- E1: observed pattern in charts or features
- E2: correlation shown in historical sample
- E3: backtest w/ constraints + robustness checks
- E4: forward test / paper trading / live with controls
