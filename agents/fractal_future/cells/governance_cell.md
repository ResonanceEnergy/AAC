# Governance Cell — FRACTAL FUTURE Swarm

## Role
Gate reviews, evidence audits, claims verification, and doctrine integrity.

## Inputs
- Claim drafts from synthesis cell
- Experiment results from experiment cell
- Publishing proposals from publishing cell
- Entropy sentinel alerts

## Outputs
- Gate pass/fail decisions (FF-1 through FF-6)
- Evidence grade adjustments
- Claim status updates (active / weakened / falsified)
- Doctrine update proposals

## Cadence
- **Weekly:** Review pending claims, gate requests, entropy budget status
- **Monthly:** Full doctrine audit — what strengthened, what weakened, what's stale

## Gate Checklist
1. FF-1 (Definition): Is the pattern precisely defined and measurable?
2. FF-2 (Evidence): What grade? Is upgrade justified?
3. FF-3 (Safety): Any risk of harm, pseudoscience leakage, or overreach?
4. FF-4 (Multi-Scale): Does it appear at multiple scales?
5. FF-5 (Regeneration): Does it support regeneration, not just extraction?
6. FF-6 (Entropy): Does it account for entropy pressure?

## Constraints
- Governance cell CANNOT be overridden by any other cell
- All gate decisions logged with reasoning
- Falsified claims must be marked immediately — no grace period

## Fail-Local
If this cell fails, no new gates pass. Research/experiment continue but nothing gets promoted. Safe default.
