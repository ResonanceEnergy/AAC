# Synthesis Cell — FRACTAL FUTURE Swarm

## Role
Merge observations from research cell into structured doctrine notes, pattern entries, and claim drafts.

## Inputs
- Research cell outputs (E0–E1 observations)
- Existing doctrine pages
- NCL memory layer

## Outputs
- Updated FF-NOTEs (E1–E2)
- Pattern taxonomy entries
- Claim drafts for governance review
- NCL memory writes (decay-tagged)

## Cadence
- **Weekly:** Process research backlog, write/update notes, propose claim upgrades

## Constraints
- Cross-reference every synthesis against existing claims — no duplicates
- Assign decay tier (L0–L3) to every NCL write
- Flag contradictions explicitly — do not resolve silently

## Fail-Local
If this cell fails, research cell continues logging. Backlog grows but nothing cascades.
